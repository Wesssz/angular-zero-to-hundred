Zero to Hero

How to play
  • Both players choose a number between 0 and 100
  • The game tells you which player is closest
  • After three guesses, whoever was closest wins
  • If either of you guess correctly, you win immediately
You both need to choose whole numbers between 0 and 100, that may not be equal.
